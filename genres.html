Music Genre Mind Map

A living family tree of popular music — the genres, how they descend from and cross-pollinate one another, the artists who defined them, and the countries where they were born. Click any node to read a summary, a deeper explanation, its key artists, and everything it connects to. Drag nodes to rearrange, scroll to zoom, drag the background to pan, and use the filters to isolate genres, artists, or countries.

Select a node to see a summary, a deeper explanation, its key artists, and connections. Start with the blue hub in the center, one of the green genres around it, or a purple country — or use the filters and search to explore.

Genre lineage is genuinely debatable — this is an informed, opinionated sketch rather than a definitive taxonomy, meant as a fun way to explore how the music connects. Artist and country links point to the most iconic examples, not an exhaustive list.
