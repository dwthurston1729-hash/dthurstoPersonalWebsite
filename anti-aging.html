Anti-Aging Research Mind Map

An interactive map of the most promising avenues of human longevity research — the biological levers scientists are pulling to slow, stop, or reverse aging — connected to the researchers driving them and the landmark studies and books behind each idea. Click any node to read about it and jump to its sources. Drag nodes to rearrange, scroll to zoom, and drag the background to pan.

Drag · scroll to zoom · click a node

Select a node to see details, sources, and connections. Start with the blue hub in the center, or one of the green research avenues around it.

Studies link to their DOI (permanent). Books link to a web search. This map is a personal learning tool, not medical advice — many interventions shown are supported chiefly by animal or early human data. Framework grounded in López-Otín et al., “Hallmarks of Aging: An Expanding Universe,” Cell (2023).
